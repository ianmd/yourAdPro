Frequently Asked Questions

What is domain authority?

Domain authority is metric used for search engine ranking. The higher a site has domanin authority, the more better it will rank on search engines.

Do links from high domain authority sites help my ranking?

Yes. This links from high domain authority websites send signals to search engine that your site has valuable information. If you have a lot of good links like that, your ranks will improve in the search results

Can I approve the blog post before you post it?

No. Guest post is highly dependant on the proper adherence to guidelines provided by authority sites. In this case, we try to meet or exceed the expectation of the authority site. In this endeavor, we are unable to get your approval. However, we do our best to keep the relevance to your products as much as we can.

Can I link to any page on my site?

No. The authority sites almost always insist on linking to a valuable content for the sake of their own audiences. For this reason, we ensure that the quality of content on your site is top-notch.

What is outreach?

We find the most relevant authority sites for our targeted keyword. We then reach out to these sites and pitch a blog post that is useful for their own audience. Once they agree to publish it, we write a post for them and strategically place the keyword in that blog post. This keyword is linked back to a valuable content on your site

Do you guarantee the traffic?

Traffic estimates are dependant on the targeted domain authority sites. We do not guarantee a certain number but based on past performance of the authority site, we provide you with an expected traffic estimate.

What is the turnaround time?

Guest post service can take upto 60 days from initial order. Guest posting involves a lot of steps and moving parts that can cause delay. We try not to push the authority sites to accept a pitch. Afterall, the quality is our main concern and we are always willing to wait for the right oppurtunity.
