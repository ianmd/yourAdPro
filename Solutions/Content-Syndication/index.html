Frequently Asked Questions

What is Content Syndication Service?

Content syndication is distribution of your content to high authority content publishing sites.

Can I link back to my site in my content?

Yes. We highly encourage you to do so. This helps in bringing back a lot of publisher's traffic to your site

Can I use Content Syndication for new content?

Yes. You can use brand new content for syndication. This has it's own advantages. You can leverage the high site authority of the publishing site and even rank on google.

Will google penalize me for duplicate content?

We can not control how search engines will interpret the content. However, we take all the measures to prevent this. For example, we check if the original content is indexed by google, we encourage publishing sites to use rel=canonical tags and recommend you to change the content slightly.

Will all the backlinks improve my SEO?

No. Some sites use "no follow" links which will prevent you from getting any SEO benefits. The good news is some sites may choose to use a "do follow" link, which may improve your rank.

What if my content is not published?

We have money back gurantee. We make all the efforts to distribute your content in the most viable format. But if your content is not published, we will give you a 100 % refund.

What is the turnaround time?

It can take up to 4 weeks. However when we review your content, we recommend the correct timing for that particular content for maximum benefit.
