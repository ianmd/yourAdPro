Frequently Asked Questions

Can I order this service online?

No. Our process involves dedicated research of your business before we start writing anything. This helps us understand your business and determine your target market. Based on our research, we make recommendations about the kind of posts and frequency that will give you the best results. You are not obligated to follow our recommendations or frequency to work with us. You can sign up or send us an email to get in touch with us.

Can I pick the topics?

Yes! We would love to hear your ideas and write an engaging blog post around it. If we feel that your topic is suited for some other time or requires some changes for best results, we will let you know.

Can I approve the posts?

Yes! We send you a draft once we are finished writing. You can approve the draft or request changes.

What format will be the blog posts?

We deliver each blog post in a clean HTML format. We also offer to post it directly to your website free of charge.

Do you have expertise in our industry?

Yes! Our dedicated research process determines your niche and assigns you a writer who has expertise in your industry.

Do I have to optimize the content for SEO before posting?

No, Our blog posts come Search Engine Optimized out of the box. When we say get content without lifting a finger, we really mean it. Regardless of your blogging platform or CMS, we optimize title, description and URL.

Will you include the keywords I want?

Yes. If you have keyword suggestions, we would create the content around it. We believe stuffing keywords in irrelevant content impacts negatively on SEO. Based on your industry and your current market positioning, we will make recommendation of keywords that will give you the best results. These few keywords will always be on our minds when we write anything for you.

What is the turn around time?

Depending on the project, it could take up to 2 weeks for delivery per post. You can request for a shorter turn around time if your post is time sensitive.

Can you post on a preset schedule?

Yes. If you subscribe to our blog writing service, we will ask you for exact dates and times for posting. For SEO purposes, we recommend you post content on a schedule.
