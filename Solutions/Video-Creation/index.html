Frequently Asked Questions

What is a voiceover video?

We take written content and convert it into a short script that can be read within 4 minutes. We create a video with voice narration and relevant pictures and graphs. You can also request to add custom photos in the videos that we create.

What is a spokesperson/talking-head video?

We take written content and convert it into a short script that can be read within 4 minutes. We create a video with narration by a professional artist. We also add relevant pictures and graphs. You can request to add custom photos in the videos that we create.

What is an Enhanced video?

Enhanced video is a combination of voiceover and spokesperson video. One voiceover artist narrates the video while other spokesperson appear in the video at different times.

What do I need to get started

You can provide us with your existing content or ask us to write one for you. You can also add relevant images, graphs, video-clips or other media that you want included in your video.

Will the video have my company logo?

You can send us your logo or other design assets to include in your videos.

Can you make longer videos?

Yes. The example packages shown here are for reference only. You can customize your video order for any specific length or number of artists involved.

Can I ask for male or female artist for my videos?

Yes. You can ask for any gender in your videos suitable for the project.
