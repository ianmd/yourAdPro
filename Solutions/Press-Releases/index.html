Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Press Release Service?

When you have a newsworthy event or if you're launching a new product, a press release will help spread the word to news hungry journalists. This will make your customers aware about you and potentially increase your web traffic.

Will Press Release boost my SEO?

No. After people started abusing press releases to unethically gain SEO, google started to be ignore and sometimes penalize sites doing it. Therefore, we only use press releases to promote organically and don't use it as a link building tool.

Will Press Release hurt my SEO?

No. We don't use press releases as a link building tool and all the links are "no-follow" links. This means, search engines will get a signal to not follow these links for search rankings. This will tell search engines that you are not trying to spam or use it unethically.

Do I need to organize an event to take advantage of this?

No. When you work with us, we will craft a game plan to make your product or services newsworthy. We will use this as an event to strategically use press releases.

Can I approve the press release?

Yes. We will send you the draft before distributing to the news sites so you know exactly what is going to be published.

What is the turnaround time?

It can take up to 15 days depending on targeted news sites.
