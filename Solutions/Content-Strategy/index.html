Frequently Asked Questions

What is Content Strategy?

A content strategy is careful planning of content creation and publishing targeted towards a particular audience to achieve a specific goal. Our content strategy service will help you achieve authority and gain more organic leads over time.

How do you develop a content strategy?

We consider the following factors in developing your content strategy.

  • - Your existing Content
  • - Your competitors content
  • - Your voice and authority
  • - Market Research
  • - Current trends
  • - Performance Metrics

Once we evaluate all the factors, we set a goal and create an editorial calendar. We create a series of content that adresses all the gaps in your content and most sought after industry topics.

Does your content strategy include social media and emails?

Yes. Our content strategy is designed to carry your messaging across all your channels. This helps ensure that your customers and prospects recieve a unified experience. This also helps in reclaiming your brand voice and authority so your customers can remember you whereever they see your content.

Do you measure the results?

Yes! Our content strategy service comes with a reporting system that keeps you informed on what works best. We also use these measurements in future content development.
