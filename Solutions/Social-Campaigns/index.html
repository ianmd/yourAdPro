Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Social Media Campaign?

Social Media Campaigns are highly goal oriented content campaigns that run on different social media like facebook, instagram, linkedin, twitter, etc. These campaigns are run in a series of small bits of content that can be easily consumed within a short attention span of your prospects. Our campaigns are tailored to specific goals that we set in the beginning of the campaign. These goals can be improvement in brand awareness, web traffic or engagements like likes and shares

Are these campaigns promotional?

No. These campaigns deliver valuable content for your followers or prospects.

Are you going to post on my social pages?

Yes. Based on your contract with us, we will require access to your social pages and post based on previously agreed upon schedules.

Will you post the same content on different social media?

No. We will create content suitable for each social network based on your niche and current standing. However, we may repurpose parts of content to achieve coherent messaging.

Will you post only text content?

No. We will create shareable content in the form of rich images and videos in combination with texts. Even if a content is focused on text, we will include graphics that will help us engage more people.

Will I be able to approve content before posting?

Yes. Each post will require your approval. However, for convenience, we will have your approval for a bunch of posts ahead of time. This will help us keep the messaging coherent while keeping you in the loop.
