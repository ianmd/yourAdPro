Frequently Asked Questions

Can I order case studies online?

Yes. If you have worked with us before, email your account manager to get access to order portal. If you are new, contact us to set up an account with us.

Do I need to give you ideas for a case study?

No. We will study your business and recommend to you how you can narrow down target product or service for a case study.

Can I approve the Case study draft?

Yes. We send you a draft once we are done writing. You can approve the draft or request changes.

Will it be formatted to be posted online?

When we make recommendations for a case study, we will also suggest how you should format it. Regardless of our recommendation, the case study will be formatted in the way you would like it. Our case studies come formatted with graphics ready to be posted on your website.

How do you conduct a client interview?

We find the clients best suited for the case-study subject from your client-history or reviews. We then contact them on your behalf for a phone or skype interview to share their experience with you. Based on your client's preference, we email them interview questions in advance to ensure our subject is covered thoroughly.

Are case studies SEO optimized?

Not really. The focus of a case study is not SEO. We are very careful with our SEO practices to avoid any potential penalties from search engines. You can order a promotional blog posts and/or videos that are SEO optimized for each of your case studies.

What is a promotional blog-post?

A promotional blog post will increase your case study's exposure by linking it a relevant topic. Depending on the subject of your case study, we will write a pursuasive blog post that will guide the targeted readers to your case study.

What is a promotional video?

A promotional video will be either a short video version of your case study or a different useful content for your target audience posted on youtube. Like the promotional blog posts, these videos will drive traffic to your case study.

What is the turn around time?

Depending on the project, it could take up to 4 weeks for delivery.
