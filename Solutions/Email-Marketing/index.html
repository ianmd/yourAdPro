Frequently Asked Questions

What is an Email Campaign?

Email Campaigns are targeted content campaigns that are delivered in your subscribers inboxes. These campaigns are goal oriented and deliver informative content to your prospects to help them in their buying journey. These campaigns are carefully designed to be digested in small bits.

Are these campaigns promotional?

No. These campaigns deliver valuable content for your subscribers or customers. However, you have the oppurtunity to combine these with your promotional campaings.

Do you provide a CRM software?

No. You can use any CRM you like. We will deliver the ready-to-go content to you with schedule so you can run the campaign. Based on our contract, we can also do this for you.

Will your email be text based?

No. We will provide you with fully designed html pages that will also include graphics.
